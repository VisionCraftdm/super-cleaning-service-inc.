Our Services

Super Cleaning Services, Inc. provides many commercial services such as office cleaning, janitorial services, and floor care. We offer monthly, weekly, bi-weekly, or custom cleaning schedules. Here is a partial list of what we do:

  • Vacuum, sweep, and damp mop floors as needed
  • Clean/disinfect sinks, toilets, urinals
  • Polish fixtures, dispensers, and streak-free mirrors
  • Clean/disinfect handles and door pulls
  • Refill soap, towel, and tissue dispensers
  • Dust and wet mop floors with registered/neutral germicide
  • Attention to under/around toilet bases
  • Collect trash and replace liners
  • Acid treat urinals
  • Vacuum carpeted areas and under desks
  • High dust areas (lighting, vents)
  • Clean corners, edges, ceilings for cobwebs
  • Wipe tables and desks (papers left on desks remain undisturbed)
  • Clean in, around, and under vending machines
  • Clean walls and doors for smudges
  • Other office cleaning and janitorial services as specified
